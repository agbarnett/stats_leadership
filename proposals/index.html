Expressions of interest

We are currently seeking expressions of interest to attend the event; a two-day symposium aiming to build statistical leadership skills among Early and Mid-Career Statisticians across Australia. We welcome statisticians from all sectors, including research organisations, government and industry. We are aiming to keep registration costs as low as possible, and will be offering opportunities to apply for travel bursaries to support attendance.
