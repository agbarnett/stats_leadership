Expressions of interest

We are currently seeking expressions of interest to attend the event. We welcome statisticians from all sectors, including research organisations, government and industry.

Please use the Google form here to submit your expression of interest. Submissions will towards the end of March.

Travel bursaries

We have funding for 10 travel bursaries for participants who wish to attend in-person at either Brisbane or Melbourne and need financial assistance. Applications will open soon.

Please choose the city closest to your home destination or make a strong case for travelling to the farther city, e.g., to attend ICOTS.

If we receive more than 10 eligible applications, the winners will be chosen at random.
