Accessibility

All event spaces are fitted with standard AV equipment and are fully wheelchair accessible.

We welcome attendees with children. The nearest baby change facility is on Level 3 of X-block near the entrance near lifts. Amenities include armchair, sink and baby change station. We are happy to help you find this facility.
