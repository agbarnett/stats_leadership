Call for abstracts

We are inviting submissions to present at our inaugural symposium on Essential skills for statistics leadership in consultancy and research. Abstracts for short oral presentations will be open until 16th April 2026 and delivered in person at either the Brisbane or Melbourne location.

Instructions

Please complete our Google form to submit your abstract.

We will accept abstracts which have clear aims and clear relevance to the aims and themes of the symposium.

The four broad themes for the symposium are:

  1. Cross-disciplinary communication
  2. Building a leadership profile
  3. Transitioning from team contributor to team leader
  4. Sustainable models for research consultancy

Please note that we are only able to accept one submission for an oral presentation per participant, although participants can be named as co-authors on other submissions.
